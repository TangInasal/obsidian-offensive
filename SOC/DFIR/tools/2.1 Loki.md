Loki is THOR’s open-source sibling. 
It is a straightforward IOC and YARA scanner that checks a system against known indicators of compromise. 
THOR is a full compromise assessment platform, 
Loki is deliberately focused: it scans files, processes, and specific system locations against signature databases and reports matches.

Loki ships with a curated set of YARA rules and hash-based IOCs. 
It checks for filename patterns associated with known attacker tools, hashes of known malicious files, YARA rule matches against file contents, and process anomalies such as suspicious parent-child relationships.
